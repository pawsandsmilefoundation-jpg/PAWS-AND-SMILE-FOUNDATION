Welcome to Paws and Smile Foundation

Spreading kindness and compassion to animals in need. Together we can create a world where every animal is loved and cared for.

About Us

Paws and Smile Foundation is dedicated to rescuing, protecting, and providing shelter to stray and abandoned animals. Our mission is to create awareness about empathy and animal welfare. We conduct adoption drives, rescue operations, and awareness campaigns to bridge the gap between helpless animals and loving families. Our goal is to build a compassionate society where every life, whether human or animal, is valued.

Our Work

  • Rescue: Saving injured and abandoned animals.
  • Adoption: Finding safe and loving homes for strays.
  • Feeding Programs: Regular street feeding initiatives.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Educating communities on animal care.
